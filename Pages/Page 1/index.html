Dragon Ball Super

Dragon Ball Super is the latest installment in the iconic Dragon Ball anime franchise, and it has captured the hearts of fans all around the world.

Introduction to Dragon Ball Super

Dragon Ball Super is a Japanese anime series produced by Toei Animation. It is the sequel to the original Dragon Ball anime and takes place after the events of Dragon Ball Z. The series follows the adventures of Goku and his friends as they battle powerful foes and explore new worlds.

The plot of Dragon Ball Super revolves around Goku and his friends as they defend the universe from powerful enemies. The series introduces new characters and explores new worlds, including the multiverse.

Characters of Dragon Ball Super

The series features a large cast of characters, both new and old. Goku is the main protagonist and the most powerful warrior in the series. His friends include Vegeta, Piccolo, and Krillin, who all have their unique fighting styles and abilities.

Dragon Ball Super received mixed reviews from critics and fans. While some praised the series for its action, animation, and characters, others criticized it for its pacing, inconsistent power scaling, and lack of originality.
