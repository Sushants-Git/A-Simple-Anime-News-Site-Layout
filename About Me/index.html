About Me

Hi there! My name is Sushant Mishra and welcome to my Anime News Site.

I have been watching anime for some years now. I think it's one of the best story telling mediums. So much more can be expressed and shown in this medium then any other medium that's why i love it.

Passionate Anime Enthusiast and Blogger

I am a passionate anime enthusiast and blogger. Ever since I was a child, I have been fascinated by the art and storytelling of anime. I love exploring the worlds and characters created by anime and discovering the deeper meanings behind their narratives.

Aims and Goals of Anime Blogging: Sharing Knowledge and Building Community

As an anime blogger, I strive to share my knowledge and insights with fellow fans. I enjoy analyzing and reviewing the latest anime releases, as well as delving into classic series that have stood the test of time. My aim is to provide a unique perspective on anime that will engage and inform readers.

In addition to anime, I am also interested in Japanese culture and language. I have studied Japanese for several years and have a deep appreciation for the country's history and traditions.
